How It Works

👨‍⚕️ For Blood Donors

1
Register as Donor
Create your profile with blood group, city, and contact details. Verify your identity to join the network.
2
Stay Eligible
Ensure you're healthy, well-hydrated, and haven't donated in the last 90 days (56 days for platelets).
3
Get Notified
Receive emergency requests in your city for your blood group. Respond instantly when you can help.
4
Donate & Save
Visit the requested blood bank/hospital. Your single donation can save up to three lives.

🏥 For Requesters (Patients/Families)

1
Create Request
Log in and submit a blood request with patient details, required blood group, quantity, and hospital location.
2
Instant Matching
Our system instantly finds eligible donors in your city who are ready to donate (≥90 days since last donation).
3
Connect Directly
Get phone/email of matching donors. Contact them immediately to arrange donation at nearest blood bank.
4
Update Status
Once blood is arranged, mark request as "Fulfilled" to notify other donors and keep database clean.
